## Posting tips

- **Add a photo or short screen-recording** of the leaderboard or a tournament bracket — visual posts get far more engagement than text alone.
- **Post in the morning or early evening** when people scroll most.
- **Pin a comment** with the app link (`https://thekitchen-98uh.vercel.app`) so it's easy to find — or reply with it individually to build conversation, which the algorithm rewards.
- **Reply quickly** to every "ME" / 🏓 comment — fast replies keep the post climbing in the feed.
- Consider posting in: local pickleball Facebook groups, your club's own page, regional pickleball association groups, and "buy/sell/swap" community groups where players hang out.
- **Don't paste the raw link in the main post** if you can avoid it — Facebook tends to show link-only posts to fewer people. Put it in the first comment instead.
